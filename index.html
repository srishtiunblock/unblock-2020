Unblock: A space to interact, experience and engage

"Unblock, is an annual event that takes place at Srishti Institute of Art Design and Technology where students in the BVoc space come together to exchange ideas, collaborate and learn; not just from each other but from industry professionals and expert craftsmen. The three weeks of interim allow the students move beyond their chosen disciplines and try their hand in other projects. At the end of the three weeks, the students exhibit, perform, discuss or present their projects at the event which is called Unblock. "

Thollu Bommalata

We explore the traditional craft of shadow puppetry from Andra Pradesh and immerse ourselves in all the different aspects of the art. This year, despite being spread throughout the country we work in tandem with the artisans to create something beautiful and worthwhile.

What would the world look like in 2030? How would we as practitioners and designers be working and what ideas would we be working on? Unblock 2019 prods and explores these questions, as we enter a new decade.
